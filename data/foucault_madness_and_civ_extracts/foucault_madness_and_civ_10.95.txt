The 
Erinnyes are recalled to that darkness which is their own—their 
birthplace and their truth, i.e., their own nothingness.Venez-vous m’enlever dans reternelle nuitf 

(Do you come to bear me off into eternal night?)This is the moment when it is revealed that the images of 
madness are only dream and error, and if the sufferer who is 
blinded by them appeals to them, it is only to disappear with 
them in the annihilation to which they are fated.A second time, then, we pass through a circle of night.But we 
are not thereby restored to the daylight reality of the world.
